## Agentic AI Framework for Business Model Innovation, Deployment Strategy, Funding Roadmap, and Commercialization of SADRIS

**Develop an AI-powered strategic planning ecosystem that autonomously generates and evaluates business models, deployment strategies, funding opportunities, sustainability pathways, and commercialization plans for the SADRIS platform. Multiple AI agents collaborate to analyze market trends, stakeholder requirements, regulatory frameworks, investment opportunities, and societal impact to create adaptive, scalable go-to-market strategies.**

| | |
|---|---|
| **Skills** | Generative AI, Agentic AI, Business Analytics, Innovation Management, Entrepreneurship, Strategic Management, Market Intelligence, Financial Modeling, Data Analytics |
| **Inputs** | Disaster management market reports, government policies, startup ecosystem data, grant databases, ESG frameworks, smart city initiatives, stakeholder requirements, investment trends |
| **Outputs** | AI-generated business models, deployment strategies, funding recommendations, commercialization roadmaps, sustainability metrics |
| **Deliverables** | Business Model Canvas Generator, AI-based Funding Discovery Engine, Commercialization Strategy Framework, Sustainability Assessment Platform, Investor Readiness Report, Go-to-Market Strategy Document |
